IndicVisionBench

A Framework and Benchmark for Indic Multimodal LLM Evaluation
Rishikant Chigrupaatii, Ponnada Sai Tulasi Kanishka, Lalit Chandra Routhu, Martin Patel Sama Supratheek Reddy, Divyam Gupta, Rajiv Misra, Rohun Tripathi
Code Dataset Leaderboard arXiv

We present IndicVisionBench, a scalable benchmark and dataset creation framework for evaluating Vision-Language Models (VLMs) in Hindi and Telugu. Covering real-world, cultural, STEM, and reasoning tasks, it combines human-verified, culturally grounded Indic QA sets with adapted English datasets. We reveal performance regressions of up to 15.6 points when evaluating SOTA models in Indic languages versus English, underscoring the urgent need for multilingual VLM robustness.

Our benchmark reveals consistent performance drops for state-of-the-art models like GPT-4.1 and Gemini 2.5 Flash when evaluated in Hindi and Telugu compared to English across four out of five subsets.

We showcase qualitative examples for different domains in our dataset.
Please see our released dataset for all samples.
